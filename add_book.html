Tutorial

Send an email

To post a new book to Page Turners, simply send an email using the button below.

Please add your username (note that the username will be permanently linked to your email address), the name of the book, the genre and a clear image of the cover.
Apart from that, add a description (about 50 words) and a review of the book (about 150 words). Feel free to add a more detailed review if you wish. Lastly, please add a rating out of 5 stars, with 5 being the best, 1 the worst.
You will receive an email once your book has been added.
